16 FAQ

Clear answers,
before you begin.

Still have questions? Talk to our team →

Is WhatsJet based on the official WhatsApp API?+

Yes. WhatsJet is built to work with WhatsApp Business Platform functionality, including approved templates, webhooks and business messaging workflows.

Can I run bulk WhatsApp campaigns?+

Yes. You can create segmented audiences, select approved templates, schedule campaigns and track delivery performance.

Can my sales team use the same WhatsApp inbox?+

Yes. Assign conversations to agents, add labels and notes, and give everyone the full context required to respond well.

How does AI automation work?+

Build automated response flows, add knowledge for AI-assisted replies, and configure rules for when to transfer conversations to a human.

Do Meta charges apply separately?+

Yes. WhatsApp Business Platform conversation and template fees are determined and billed according to Meta’s current pricing policies.
